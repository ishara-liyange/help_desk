Histiry


Our online help desk for university students was established in 1998 with the aim of providing comprehensive support to students navigating their academic journey. Foundation: The idea stemmed from recognizing the challenges students face in accessing timely assistance and resources. With this in mind, a dedicated team came together to create a centralized platform for addressing these needs. Evolution: Over the years, the help desk has evolved to meet the changing demands of students, continuously expanding its services and refining its approach based on user feedback and emerging trends in higher education. Impact: Since its inception, the help desk has served thousands of students, making a tangible difference in their academic success and overall well-being.

Our Vision


Empowering Students: Our vision is to empower university students by providing them with the tools, support, and guidance they need to excel academically and thrive personally. Creating a Supportive Community: We envision a community where students feel valued, supported, and inspired to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

our Mission


Mission Statement: Our mission is to provide university students with accessible, reliable, and personalized support to help them overcome academic challenges, achieve their goals, and realize their full potential. Core Values: At the heart of our mission are values such as integrity, empathy, and inclusivity, which guide our interactions with students and drive our commitment to excellence in everything we do.
